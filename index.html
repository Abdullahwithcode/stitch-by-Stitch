About Us

Stitch by Stitch specializes in CMT (Cut, Make & Trim) / overhead stitching for men’s and women’s garments. We support boutiques and brands with reliable bulk production, consistent quality, and on-time delivery.

Our skilled team is dedicated to bringing your designs to life with precision and care. Whether you're a small boutique or a growing brand, we tailor our services to meet your unique needs.
