Our Story

Founded in Karachi, Stitch by Stitch grew from a small sample-room workshop into a trusted CMT partner for international brands. We combine traditional tailoring expertise with modern production workflows to deliver consistent, export-ready garments at scale.

We focus on transparent communication, strict quality checks and flexible production runs — from low-volume sampling to full bulk orders. Our team supports private-label production, development, costing and compliant packing for overseas shipments.
