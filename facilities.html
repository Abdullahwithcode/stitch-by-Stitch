Factory Overview

Our modern facility spans 25,000 square feet of dedicated manufacturing space in SITE Industrial Area, Karachi. The factory is equipped with climate-controlled production areas to maintain consistent quality and worker comfort.

With strategic layout and organization, we optimize workflow efficiency while maintaining strict quality standards across all production areas.
